The Problem

Sound familiar?

Four systems. Zero agreement.

Stock in one spreadsheet. Orders in another. Warehouse locations in a third. Your WMS and your accounting package have never met.

Wrong orders. Missed orders. Lost trust.

Marketplace orders arriving in the wrong warehouse. Items shipped that aren’t in stock. Returns you didn’t see coming. Each error is a customer you won’t get back.

Flying blind on replenishment.

No clear view of what’s on each shelf, which warehouse holds which stock, or what to reorder before it runs out. Your 45-day lead times don’t forgive guesswork.

These aren’t software problems. They’re what happens when you’ve outgrown the tools you started with.

Still Independently Owned

While our competitors were being acquired, we were getting better at what we do.

Stok.ly has not been acquired. We are not being optimised for exit. We are backed by Midven and run by the people who built the platform. Our customers have a first-name relationship with the people who answer the phone.

We intend to keep it that way.

The Platform

One system for your entire operation.

From goods-in to despatch. Warehouse floor to customer doorstep. B2C and B2B in the same platform.

Warehouse Management

Barcode scanning via Android app. Multi-location bin inventory. Paperless pick, pack, and despatch. Real-time stock movements with full audit trails by user and timestamp.

  • Barcode scanning with Android warehouse app
  • Multi-warehouse bin management
  • Automated pick-face replenishment
  • 70+ courier and freight integrations

Order Management

Pull orders from Shopify, WooCommerce, Amazon, eBay, and B2B channels. Automatically allocated, routed, and tracked. Backorders, pre-orders, and proforma invoicing handled without manual intervention.

  • 200+ ecommerce integrations
  • B2B trade pricing and account management
  • Automated invoicing on despatch
  • Pre-allocation to inbound purchase orders

AI Demand Forecasting

Demand prediction, automated purchase order generation, and intelligent stock balancing across locations. Configurable to your lead times, minimum stock levels, and supplier constraints.

  • AI-generated purchase orders
  • Stock transfer recommendations
  • Pick-face replenishment by pick rate
  • Manufacturing demand planning

Manufacturing & Kitting

Manage raw material inventory and finished goods in the same platform. Build kits and bundles whose available quantity is calculated in real time from component stock and pushed live to every connected sales channel.

  • Bills of materials (BOMs)
  • Component inventory management
  • Kitted bundle stock pushed to ecommerce
  • Yield and reject rate reporting
